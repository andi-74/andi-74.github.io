Normal Raid

At the beginning of each season, we learn the fights on normal together and progress through the raid until the majority of players no longer need normal boss loot.

Normal Raid, Continued

Once we start heroic, we dedicate a second night to continue normal raid. Alts and new players are welcome to join us in a more casual setting.

Heroic Raid

Heroic-ready players can sign up for the heroic raid. Raiders who learn the boss mechanics and hit performance targets are invited to earn AOTC with us.
